Born: November 28, 2007 | Hometown: Golagodu, near Sagar, Karnataka

Early Life & Inspiration

Growing up in the small village of Golagodu, Samartha GS (widely known as Epic or Sammu) discovered his passion for technology at a very young age. His curiosity for how things work led him to dismantle household gadgets and rebuild them with creative twists. During the COVID-19 pandemic, he dived deep into IoT (Internet of Things), completing over 50+ projects ranging from smart home systems to AI-powered automation tools.

He credits much of his inspiration to his sister, Supriya Bhatt GS, who encouraged him to take risks, learn coding, and apply his knowledge in meaningful ways. This support ignited his transition from hardware tinkering to professional-level software development.

Tech Journey

Samartha started his web journey in 2021 with a basic Blogspot site. From there, he taught himself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and gradually began creating calculators, timers, and utility-based apps. With persistence, he moved on to advanced development such as PHP-based apps, full-stack games, and cloud-integrated platforms.

His progress was shared with his peers and family, earning appreciation for his consistency and creativity. By 2022, he launched his portfolio website and began building software solutions for practical use.

Major Projects

Some of his standout projects include:

  • AI Chatbot – a conversational assistant inspired by ChatGPT with real-world API integration.
  • MyWebSam – a free, watermark-free profile page builder using Firebase (like Linktree).
  • Tic Tac Toe Multiplayer – a Node.js real-time online game connecting players worldwide.
  • Echolink – a social app for comments and media sharing.
  • Custom Tools – including search engines and quote generators.

Beyond Technology

Apart from his coding journey, Samartha is an active volleyball player, often playing as a setter in school and district-level tournaments. He believes balancing sports and technology strengthens both his physical endurance and problem-solving mindset.

Vision for the Future

Looking ahead, Samartha GS envisions becoming a leading force in AI, cloud, and full-stack development. His long-term mission is to build platforms that simplify technology for everyday users, making digital tools accessible, engaging, and impactful. He aspires to not just build apps but also inspire young minds to explore innovation without fear of failure.
